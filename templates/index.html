How It Works

1. Post Item

Report lost or found items with details and location.

2. Claim Item

Users can claim items with verification message.

3. Get It Back

Owner verifies and returns the item safely.


Our College Lost & Found Portal is designed to simplify the process of reconnecting lost items with their rightful owners. With a user-friendly interface and secure claim verification, students can easily report and recover belongings.

The system ensures transparency and safety by allowing only verified users to interact, while maintaining privacy through controlled contact sharing. It promotes a responsible and helpful campus environment.
